# Shipping and Delivery Policy

**Platform**: RepairMyBike  
**Last Updated**: 2026-03-28  
**Effective Date**: 2026-03-28

---

## 1. Introduction

This Shipping and Delivery Policy ("Policy") governs the physical delivery of spare parts and physical products purchased through the **RepairMyBike** Platform. This Policy applies exclusively to the **Spare Parts E-Commerce** module of our Platform. Repair service bookings (Home Service and Shop Visit) are governed separately under the Service Policy.

---

## 2. Applicable Products

This Policy applies to all spare parts listed in the RepairMyBike catalog, including (but not limited to):

- Engine components
- Batteries and electrical parts
- Tyres, brakes, and wheels
- Filters, lubricants, and consumables
- Body parts and accessories

---

## 3. Delivery Availability

We deliver spare parts to serviceable pincodes across India. Users are advised to verify pincode serviceability at the Checkout page before placing an order. Orders placed for unserviceable or incorrect pincodes will be cancelled and the User notified.

---

## 4. Shipping Methods and Timelines

| Shipping Method | Estimated Delivery | Availability |
|---|---|---|
| Standard Delivery | 3–5 business days | Pan-India (serviceable pincodes) |
| Express Delivery | 1–2 business days | Select metro cities |

- **Order Processing**: All orders are processed within 24 working hours of confirmation. Orders placed on public holidays or weekends are processed on the next business day.
- **Delays**: The Company is not liable for delivery delays caused by logistics partner failures, natural calamities, force majeure events, or incorrect address information provided by the User.
- Where delays are anticipated, the User will be notified via Push Notification (Firebase) and/or WhatsApp (where configured).

---

## 5. Shipping Charges

- Shipping charges are calculated dynamically at checkout based on the total order weight, package dimensions, shipping method selected, and destination pincode.
- All applicable shipping fees are clearly disclosed on the final checkout screen before order confirmation.
- There may be promotional periods with free shipping above a minimum order value, as communicated on the Platform.

---

## 6. Order Tracking

Once a spare part order is shipped:

1. A **Tracking Number** and **Courier Partner Name** are updated in the Platform under the order details.
2. A direct **Courier Tracking URL** is provided where available.
3. The User receives a Push Notification (Firebase) upon shipment.
4. Real-time shipment updates (hub-wise movement, out for delivery, delivered) are logged within the Platform's "Order History" section.

---

## 7. Proof of Delivery

Delivery is considered complete when the courier partner's system reflects a "Delivered" status. Depending on the courier's process, delivery may require:

- A signature from the recipient.
- A delivery OTP confirmation.

The Company recommends that Users or an authorized representative be available at the delivery address during the expected delivery window.

---

## 8. Risk of Loss and Title Transfer

The risk of loss and title for purchased spare parts transfer to the User upon handover to the third-party logistics (3PL) partner. However, RepairMyBike will actively assist in resolution if an item is confirmed lost in transit by the courier partner.

---

## 9. Damaged or Tampered Shipments

If a package is visibly tampered or damaged upon arrival:

1. **Refuse Acceptance** — Do not accept the delivery from the courier agent.
2. **Document the Damage** — Take photographs of the packaging before opening.
3. **Report Within 24 Hours** — Contact our support team at support@repairmybike.in with your Order ID and photographs.

Damaged items reported after 24 hours of delivery may not be eligible for replacement or refund.

---

## 10. Failed Deliveries and Returns to Origin (RTO)

If a delivery fails due to:

- An incorrect or incomplete address provided by the User.
- The User being unavailable for multiple delivery attempts.
- Refusal to accept the delivery.

The package will be returned to our warehouse (Return to Origin / RTO). A redelivery can be arranged subject to additional shipping charges. If the User does not request redelivery within 7 days of RTO, the order may be cancelled.

---

## 11. Order Modifications After Placement

- **Address Changes**: Address modifications are not permitted after the order is marked "Shipped".
- **Item Modifications**: Items cannot be added or removed from an order after placement. Users must place a new order.
- To cancel an order before shipment, please refer to the Refund and Cancellation Policy.

---

## 12. Payment on Delivery

> **⚠️ CURRENT STATUS**: RepairMyBike currently operates as a **Cash-Only** platform. All spare part orders are placed on a **Cash on Delivery (COD)** basis. The delivery agent will collect the payment in cash upon successful delivery. Online prepayment via UPI, cards, or net banking is **not currently supported**.

---

## 13. Contact

For all shipping and delivery queries:

- **Email**: support@repairmybike.in
- **Response Time**: Within 2 business days.
