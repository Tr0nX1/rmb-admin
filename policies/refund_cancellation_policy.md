# Refund and Cancellation Policy

**Platform**: RepairMyBike  
**Last Updated**: 2026-03-28  
**Effective Date**: 2026-03-28

---

## 1. Objective

At **RepairMyBike**, we are committed to a transparent and fair service experience. This Policy ("Policy") outlines the terms governing cancellations of repair bookings and spare part orders, and the processing of refunds thereunder.

---

## 2. Current Payment Method

> **⚠️ IMPORTANT**: RepairMyBike currently operates as a **Cash-Only** platform. Online payment methods (credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking via Razorpay or any other payment gateway) are **not active**. All transactions are settled in cash at the point of service delivery or product receipt. As a result, refunds are processed as **manual cash reimbursements or in-platform credit**, not as gateway-initiated reversals.

---

## 3. Service Booking Cancellations

### 3.1 User-Initiated Cancellation

Users may cancel bookings in the following states: **Pending** or **Confirmed** (before a technician has been dispatched or the booking is marked "In Progress").

| Cancellation Timing | Charge |
|---|---|
| More than 2 hours before appointment | No cancellation fee |
| Within 2 hours of appointment window | A "Convenience Fee" may apply to cover technician dispatch costs |
| After technician is "In Progress" | Booking cannot be cancelled; service charges apply |

### 3.2 No-Show Policy

If our technician arrives at the registered service address and the vehicle or the User is unavailable:

- A **No-Show Fee** will be recorded against the booking.
- The booking will be marked as Cancelled.
- The No-Show Fee is payable in cash or deducted from any advance payment made.

### 3.3 Company-Initiated Cancellation

If RepairMyBike is unable to fulfill a booking due to technician unavailability, safety concerns, or unforeseen operational issues:

- The User will be notified immediately via Push Notification (Firebase) and/or WhatsApp (Kapso, if configured).
- The User will be offered the option to reschedule at no additional cost, or a full refund of any advance amount paid.

---

## 4. Spare Part Order Cancellations

Cancellations for spare part orders are permitted only if the order is in one of the following statuses:

- `Created`
- `Confirmed`
- `Processing`

Once an order reaches **Shipped**, **Out for Delivery**, or **Delivered** status, it cannot be cancelled. Users in such cases must follow the Return process as described in Section 6.

---

## 5. Refund Provisions

Refund eligibility is determined as follows:

### 5.1 For Repair Service Bookings

| Scenario | Refund Outcome |
|---|---|
| Company-initiated cancellation | Full refund of any advance payment |
| Service not rendered / technician did not arrive | Full refund — manual processing by staff |
| Service quality deemed deficient (after investigation) | Partial or full refund at Company's discretion |
| Labour charges for completed services | Non-refundable |

### 5.2 For Spare Part Orders

| Scenario | Refund Outcome |
|---|---|
| Order cancelled before shipment | Full refund |
| Incorrect or damaged part received | Full refund or replacement — must be reported within **24 hours** of delivery |
| Part returned after delivery for other reasons | Subject to inspection; partial refund may apply |
| Consumables (oils, filters) opened/applied | Non-refundable |

---

## 6. Refund Processing Workflow

The Company follows a structured internal refund workflow:

1. **User raises a Refund Request** — via the "My Bookings" section or by contacting support.
2. **Staff reviews and Approves or Rejects** — within 48 hours of submission.
3. **Staff Processes the Refund** — internal team coordinates the cash reimbursement or issues an in-platform credit.
4. **Staff marks the Refund as Completed** — User is notified via Push Notification.

> Since the Platform operates on Cash-Only mode, refunds are issued as **cash-back** (returned in person or via bank transfer upon provision of valid bank details) or as **Platform Credit** applied to the User's account.

---

## 7. Refund Timelines

| Refund Type | Timeline |
|---|---|
| Cash-back (in person or bank transfer) | 3–7 business days after approval |
| Platform Credit | Credited within 24 hours of approval |
| Damaged goods / wrong part | Initiated after inspection (24–72 hours) |

---

## 8. Non-Refundable Items

The following are explicitly non-refundable:

- Labour charges for services already completed and accepted.
- Subscription fees where at least one service visit has been consumed.
- Consumable items (engine oil, brake fluid, filters) once opened or applied to a vehicle.
- Convenience or No-Show fees as described in Section 3.

---

## 9. Subscription Plan Cancellation

- Users may request cancellation of their subscription plan by contacting support.
- If cancelled before any service visit has been consumed in the current billing period, a full refund may be issued at the Company's discretion.
- If one or more visits have been consumed, the subscription fee for the consumed visits will be retained.
- The Company does not currently support automated subscription renewals via online payment. Renewal billing is manual.

---

## 10. Dispute Resolution

For refund disputes, please:

1. Raise a support ticket via "My Bookings" or email support@repairmybike.in with your Booking ID or Order ID.
2. Our team will respond within 2 business days.
3. Unresolved disputes may be escalated to our Grievance Officer.
